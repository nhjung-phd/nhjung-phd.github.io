About

Nak Hyun Jung, Ph.D. is a Research Professor (IPS) and Visiting Professor (aSSIST), focusing on AI-driven finance, time-series forecasting, operations research, and large language models (LLMs). His current interests include dynamic asset allocation with AI, deep and reinforcement learning for financial markets, LLM-based decision support and automation, business-cycle indicators, and fan-driven globalization. He teaches Time-Series & AI Big-Data with an applied, hands-on approach.
