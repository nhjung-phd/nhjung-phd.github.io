About

Nak Hyun Jung, Ph.D. is a Research Professor (IPS) and Visiting Professor (aSSIST), focusing on AI-driven finance and time-series forecasting. His current interests include dynamic asset allocation with AI, deep/reinforcement learning for markets, business-cycle indicators, and fan-driven globalization. He teaches Time-Series & AI Big-Data with an applied, hands-on approach.
